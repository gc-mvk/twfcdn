Etiquette of Drinking Zamzam Water

A short educational page about drinking Zamzam water with gratitude, respect, and good manners.

Zamzam water is provided for pilgrims and worshippers in and around Masjid al-Haram.

What Is Zamzam Water?

Zamzam water is the blessed water associated with the sacred well of Zamzam in Makkah. It holds a special place in the hearts of Muslims because of its connection to Prophet Ibrahim, Hajar, and Prophet Ismail, peace be upon them.

Pilgrims often drink Zamzam after Tawaf and prayer, and many also take time to make sincere dua while drinking it. The act should be done with gratitude to Allah and awareness of the sacred surroundings.

Good Manners While Drinking

When drinking Zamzam, a pilgrim should try to be calm, clean, and respectful. It is good to say Bismillah before drinking and Alhamdulillah after finishing.

Pilgrims should avoid wasting the water, spilling it carelessly, or blocking others near the drinking areas. In crowded places, patience and consideration for other worshippers are part of good pilgrimage manners.

Dua and Reflection

Many Muslims make dua while drinking Zamzam, asking Allah for beneficial knowledge, good health, forgiveness, guidance, and acceptance of worship.

For specific rulings, recommended supplications, or detailed religious guidance about Zamzam water, pilgrims should consult qualified scholars or trusted Hajj and Umrah resources.
